 Bookkeeping is critical to any business, large or small. Without accurate and up-to-date bookkeeping, businesses would have little idea of their financial position, what money is owed to them, or what money they owe.

 Good bookkeeping also provides essential information for businesses to make informed decisions about where to allocate their resources. For example, if a business knows that it is owed a large amount of money by one of its customers, it can make the decision to continue to supply that customer on credit, safe in the knowledge that it will eventually be paid.

 Overall, bookkeeping is an essential part of running a business. It provides crucial information about a business's financial position and helps to ensure that businesses comply with tax regulations.
